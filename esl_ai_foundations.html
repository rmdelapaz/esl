Foundation Building Exercises

Exercise: AI Conversation Starter

Try this conversation with any AI chatbot:

You: "Hi! I'm learning English and would like to practice. Can you help me have a conversation about hobbies?"

AI: "Of course! I'd be happy to help you practice. What hobbies do you enjoy?"

Your turn: Respond naturally and ask the AI to correct any mistakes.

Exercise: Daily AI Check-in

For one week, start each day by telling an AI about your plans:

  • What you'll do today
  • How you're feeling
  • One thing you learned yesterday

Ask the AI to help you use new vocabulary and correct any grammar mistakes.

Exercise: Vocabulary Builder Challenge

Ask an AI to:

  1. Give you 5 new words related to your profession
  2. Use each word in a sentence
  3. Create a short story using all 5 words
  4. Quiz you on these words the next day
